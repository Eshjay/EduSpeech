About Us

We are an organisation that specialises in diction/elocution training, public speaking and communication skills, accent reduction and correct expressions/pronunciations. Our aim is to assist individuals, schools, organisations (social & religious) etc to develop an effective speaking and communication skills. We help in putting finesse to your speech through our various online and physical classes and we also employ outstanding students as facilitators/diction coaches in our organisation.

Our Services

Diction/Elocution Training

We engage in diction/elocution training, correct expressions and correct pronunciation, speak right training.

Public Speaking Training

We organise training sessions for individuals interested in building a good and effective communication skills in their various disciplines/professions.

Accent Reduction and Speech Correction

We train individuals on how to reduce influence of the natural mother tongue on their spoken English.

Translation and Transcription

We edit, translate and transcribe scripts, videos and writeups for individuals, schools and organisations

Tutorial

We engage in tutorial classes for students on English language, Literature-in-English and Yoruba language for WAEC, JAMB, GCE and other external examinations.
